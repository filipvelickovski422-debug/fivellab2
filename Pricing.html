Step-by-Step Workflow

1

Consultation

I start with a consultation to define your goals, topics, and key messages. You'll share what you want to highlight, and I'll align on the overall vision for your social media content.

2

Research & Strategy

I conduct research based on your niche and goals to determine the types of videos and posts I'll create. I then prepare a full monthly content plan and strategy to guide all upcoming work.

3

Content Preparation

Once the plan is finalized, I provide a clear outline of what needs to be filmed or photographed. You'll capture the necessary footage and images according to the plan.

4

Content Creation

I create the Reels, Stories, and static posts using your materials, ensuring all visuals are polished and aligned with the strategy. Captions and hashtags are researched and prepared to maximize reach and engagement.

5

Scheduling & Delivery

All finalized posts, Reels, Stories, captions, and hashtags are uploaded to a shared Drive folder. Posts are scheduled for publishing, giving you a clear overview and easy access to all content.
